BC

I studied at Parsons School of Design — which gives me something most people in tech don't have: a trained eye for how ideas should look and feel before they're ever understood.

In an industry full of brilliant engineers and researchers, the work doesn't land until someone translates it into something human. That's what I do. Design taught me that clarity is a creative act.

I'm drawn to AI not just as a tool but as a creative frontier — I was already concepting generative AI campaigns at Koa Skincare before it was the norm. The companies that will define this moment are the ones that can speak to both the researcher and the curious newcomer. I want to help build that voice.
